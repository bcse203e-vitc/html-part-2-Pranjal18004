Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Mix the cooked pasta into the pan. Stir well to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 350 per serving
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 55g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 4g
